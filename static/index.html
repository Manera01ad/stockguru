📖 TODAY'S LEARNING CONCEPTS
TODAY'S LESSON
5-MIN READ
CONCEPT · RSI
RSI measures if a stock is overbought (too many buyers) or oversold (too many sellers). Scale: 0–100.

🟢 RSI 40–65 = Sweet spot — normal trending
🔴 RSI > 70 = Overbought — avoid buying
🟡 RSI < 30 = Oversold — possible bounce

Today: BEL RSI ~62 → Still in buy zone.
CONCEPT · 200-DAY MOVING AVERAGE
The 200DMA is the average closing price over 200 days. Most-watched line by institutions.

📈 Price above 200DMA = Long-term uptrend — buy bias
📉 Price below 200DMA = Downtrend — avoid
💡 Cross above 200DMA = Golden signal

Today: HDFC Bank is 8% above 200DMA.
