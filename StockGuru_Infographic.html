StockGuru is a personal financial intelligence platform built for Indian retail traders. It continuously monitors NSE/BSE stocks, Gold, Silver, Crude Oil, and Crypto, scores them using live momentum algorithms, and delivers actionable BUY/WATCH/HOLD/AVOID signals directly to your phone — so you never miss a high-probability opportunity.
